# NutriLens: App Overview & Feature Set

## 1. Vision & Core Goal
**NutriLens** is a premium, deep-tech nutritional companion designed to bridge the gap between complex food labels and personal health. Unlike generic calorie counters, NutriLens uses a "Clinical-First" approach to provide hyper-personalized insights based on an individual's unique health profile, allergies, and chronic conditions.

---

## 2. Key Features

### A. Smart Scan & Cloud-Start Parallelism
*   **Instant Analysis**: Utilizes a dual-phase feedback loop. The moment OCR text is captured, it triggers a backend "Deep Scan" while simultaneously running local logic for instant "Emergency" detection.
*   **Zero-Wait Experience**: Users get immediate feedback on critical allergens or harmful ingredients while the full clinical insights are being generated by the cloud.

### B. 4+1+1 Resilience Strategy (AI Backend)
*   **100% Reliability**: A multi-layered AI fallback system ensuring the app never fails.
*   **Primary Engine**: Google Gemini 3.0 Flash.
*   **Failovers**: Seamless transition to OpenAI (GPT-4o) or OpenRouter (DeepSeek V3) if primary keys are throttled or down.

### C. The Health Bridge (Layman Engine)
*   **Technical Decoder**: Translates chemical names and INS/E numbers into simple language.
*   **Personalized "Why"**: Instead of just saying "Bad," it explains *why* it's bad for *you* (e.g., "This contains Maltodextrin, which can spike blood sugar—critical for your Pre-Diabetic profile").

### D. Indian Weighted Scoring (FSSAI+ Compliance)
*   **Contextual Grading**: A 1.0 to 10.0 scoring system specifically tuned for the Indian nutritional landscape.
*   **Weight-Based Penalty**: Ingredients listed at the top of the label (highest quantity) carry a heavier weight in the final score if they are harmful (e.g., Sugar or Palm Oil as the first ingredient).

### E. Marketing Reality Check
*   **Transparency Tool**: Automatically flags misleading front-of-pack claims. If a product claims "No Added Sugar" but contains hidden sweeteners like Maltodextrin or Invert Sugar, NutriLens highlights this discrepancy.

### F. Hyper-Personalized Health Profile
*   **Dynamic Context**: The AI factors in BMI, Fitness Goals (e.g., Shred, Bulk, Maintenance), Allergies, and Chronic Conditions (e.g., Hypertension, Diabetes) for every single scan.
*   **Integrated Tracking**: Includes a progress tracker with "Liquid Mercury" spring physics for BMI and weight monitoring.

---

## 3. UI/UX & Design Philosophy
*   **Aesthetic**: "Ethereal Clinical" / "Luminous Health Editorial" — a mix of medical precision and high-end editorial design.
*   **Liquid Glass UI**: Modern, translucent navigation bars and bento-style cards.
*   **Interactive Motion**: High-FPS spring physics, haptic feedback on "Bad" detections, and morphing shared-element transitions for a premium iOS-like feel.

---

## 4. Technical Architecture
*   **Frontend**: Flutter 3.27+ with Impeller rendering for 120Hz smoothness.
*   **Backend Integration**: Firebase for secure user data and a robust API vault for multi-LLM orchestration.
*   **Design System**: Based on Google Stitch, featuring Plus Jakarta Sans typography and 24px corner radiuses.
